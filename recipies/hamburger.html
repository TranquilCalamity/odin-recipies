Hamburger

Description

A classic hamburger with cheese, tomato, pickle, lettuce, and onion on a sesame seed bun.

Ingredients

  • hamburger patty
  • sesame seed bun
  • tomato
  • pickle
  • lettuce
  • onion
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook hamburger patty.
  2. Melt cheese on burger patty.
  3. Toast bun.
  4. Assemble burger with all ingredients.
